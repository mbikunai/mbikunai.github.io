WORKING PROJECTS

Mesh4Eye, January, 2020

Glaucoma is one of the most spreaded ocular disease. Huge amount of people suffer from this disease and most of them are unaware of that because it is an asymthomatic and hurtless disease. The aqueous humour, a sticky liquid resposnsible of nurishing and cleaning the eye, can not be drained from the eye. Thus, the intraocular pressure gets higher and higher damaging the optic nerve and consecuenlty provocking the sight loss. This project I am working on, pretends to replicate the glaucoma disease by 3D fabrication techniques, for instance electrospinning, fulfilling the biocompatibility requirements. The aim of this project is to create an in vitro 3D device able to test different drugs mimicking the ocular anatomy and physiology.
